NEXUS is a leading software platform for manufacturers and provide network better.

Our supplier intelligence services platform, NEXUS, includes modules for product sales processing, warranty claims management, parts management, service bulletins, and OEM information sharing. At the core of our platform are seamless integrations that allow OEMs to ensure data is in the right place at the right time to accelerate growth and intelligent business decision making

NEXUS was born from a need across these industries to provide manufacturers and dealers with a better software option to transact business together. Beyond our foundational modules, we provide deep insights for OEMs and their dealer partners with our advanced analytics offering.

NEXUS is a hybrid company headquartered in Grand Rapids, MI with global reach having team members across the globe. Our team culture and values are rooted in the Midwest and we are passionate to show the world why Grand Rapids is the silicon valley of our region.
